Using the Twinkle Apps Logo

Safe Space

The Twinkle Apps logo must be used with safe space. Please maintain the space when using the logo on your site or any other material. Use half the logo's width to determine the minimum amount safe space that should surround Logo.

Minimum Size

The logo “Twinkle Apps” should always be easy to read. So in digital content the Logo should never appear smaller than 24dp in height.

The minimum size for applying the Logo in print is 0.125 in/3.1 mm in height.
